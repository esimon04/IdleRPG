	=====================Equipment NOTES=======================
	*Overhaul gear type
		Gear type coincides with list of possible modifiers
	*Big 3 equipment values
		EquipmentLevel [1-100] - Determines level requirement, determines starting strength of the modifiers
			level10 = level (1-3) modifiers
			level 50 = level (4-6) modifiers
			level100 = level (8-10) modfieirs
		ItemPotential - Determines how many times item modifiers can be upgraded
		EquipmentRarity - determines number of modifiers on an item
			Common(white) - none
			Uncommon(green) - 1
			rare (blue) - 2
			epic (purple) - 3
			Legendary (yellow) - 4
	*Modifiers
		ModifierLevels [1-10] - determines strength of the modifiers
			ex.) Attribute Lvl1 = [1-3] Lvl2 = [4-6] Lvl3 =[7-9]...
			
	*Given above how does rolling for loot work
	-Enemies drop a chest, based on the enemy level
	-Opening a chest rolls
		*Rarity
		*ItemLevel
		*Modifiers (# based on rarity)
